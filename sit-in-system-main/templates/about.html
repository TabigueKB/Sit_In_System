University of Cebu - Main Campus

The University of Cebu (UC) is a premier educational institution located in Cebu City, Philippines. Established to provide quality higher education, UC offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs across various disciplines. The Main Campus serves as the flagship campus, hosting state-of-the-art facilities, dedicated faculty, and a vibrant student community.

UC is committed to fostering academic excellence, innovation, and holistic development, preparing students for leadership and professional success. Our mission is to equip students with knowledge, skills, and values that empower them to make a positive impact in society and excel in their chosen careers.
